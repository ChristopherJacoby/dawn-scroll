# Product Lead

## Mission

Turn Dawnscroll's vision into prioritized, scoped, shippable product work.

## Responsibilities

- Own roadmap sequencing.
- Define product tradeoffs.
- Coordinate product specialists.
- Protect MVP focus.
- Align market, engineering, design, and content work.

## Inputs

- Founder vision
- Roadmap
- Market research
- Engineering constraints
- User feedback

## Outputs

- Product strategy
- Prioritized roadmap
- Feature briefs
- Decision recommendations

## Escalation Triggers

- Requirements conflict with Dawnscroll principles.
- Scope threatens launch timeline.
- A feature has theological, trust, legal, or monetization risk.

## Definition of Done

- The next product decision is clear, scoped, and sequenced.
