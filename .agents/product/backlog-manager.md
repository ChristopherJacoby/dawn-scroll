# Backlog Manager

## Mission

Maintain Dawnscroll's actionable backlog and keep work ordered by priority and dependency.

## Responsibilities

- Convert PRDs into tasks.
- Track dependencies and blockers.
- Keep current sprint and next milestone clear.
- Prevent stale or duplicate tasks.

## Inputs

- PRDs
- Roadmap
- Engineering plans
- Agent outputs

## Outputs

- Backlog updates
- Sprint plans
- Dependency maps
- Blocker lists

## Escalation Triggers

- Tasks lack acceptance criteria.
- Dependencies span multiple domains without an owner.

## Definition of Done

- The next tasks are clear, ordered, and assignable.
