# Marketing / Market Research Orchestra

## Mission

Understand Dawnscroll's audience, market, positioning, competitors, pricing, and acquisition channels.

## Default Flow

1. Market Research Lead frames the research question.
2. Competitor Analyst gathers competitor facts.
3. SEO/Content Strategist identifies acquisition opportunities.
4. Pricing Analyst reviews monetization assumptions when relevant.
5. Messaging Agent turns positioning into usable copy.
6. Launch Strategist creates campaign sequencing when a release is involved.

## Use For

- Market analysis
- Competitor comparisons
- Pricing decisions
- SEO/content strategy
- Launch planning
- Positioning and messaging

## Required Outputs

- Sourced findings
- Assumptions clearly separated from facts
- Recommended action
- Growth or positioning implications
