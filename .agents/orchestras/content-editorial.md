# Content / Editorial Orchestra

## Mission

Create trustworthy, sourced, app-ready content for Roots, Hard Questions, and archaeology.

## Default Flow

1. Editorial Lead frames the content brief.
2. Roots Researcher, Hard Questions Researcher, or Archaeology Researcher builds the research foundation.
3. Source Reliability Reviewer rates sources.
4. Citation Integrity Reviewer checks claim support.
5. Fact Checker verifies factual claims.
6. Theological Sensitivity Reviewer reviews tone and framing.
7. Copy Editor prepares final prose.

## Use For

- Roots entries
- Hard Questions answers
- Archaeology evidence pages
- App content blocks
- Public-facing educational copy

## Required Outputs

- Research brief
- Source list
- Claim confidence notes
- Reviewed app-ready draft
