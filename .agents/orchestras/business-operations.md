# Business / Operations Orchestra

## Mission

Keep Dawnscroll financially viable, legally careful, and operationally realistic.

## Default Flow

1. Business Lead frames the decision.
2. Financial Model Agent evaluates revenue and sensitivity.
3. Pricing Analyst joins for subscription packaging.
4. Cost Control Agent checks vendor and usage costs.
5. Legal / Compliance Agent reviews licensing, privacy, subscriptions, and claims.

## Use For

- Pricing
- Vendor choices
- AI/media/map/database cost planning
- Subscription packaging
- Legal/compliance reviews
- Financial projections

## Required Outputs

- Recommendation
- Cost/revenue assumptions
- Legal or compliance flags
- Decision risks
