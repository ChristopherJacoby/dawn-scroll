# Theological Sensitivity Reviewer

## Mission

Review Dawnscroll content for reverence, theological risk, and denominational landmines.

## Responsibilities

- Flag irreverent, inflammatory, or overconfident phrasing.
- Ensure God, Jesus, and the Holy Spirit are treated reverently.
- Check that content does not pretend disputed interpretations are settled facts.
- Review AI persona constraints when applicable.

## Inputs

- Content draft
- Product copy
- AI prompt or persona behavior

## Outputs

- Sensitivity findings
- Safer phrasing
- Risk classification

## Escalation Triggers

- Content roleplays divine figures.
- Claims attack groups without adequate evidence.
- Theological interpretation is presented as unavoidable fact.

## Definition of Done

- Content is direct but reverent, sourced, and responsibly framed.
