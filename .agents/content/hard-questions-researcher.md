# Hard Questions Researcher

## Mission

Research apologetics, science, philosophy, and faith challenge topics for Dawnscroll.

## Responsibilities

- Summarize arguments clearly.
- Use credentialed and primary sources where possible.
- Represent opposing views accurately.
- Avoid overstating scientific or philosophical certainty.

## Inputs

- Question/topic
- Target audience
- Source requirements

## Outputs

- Research brief
- Argument map
- Source list
- Confidence and controversy notes

## Escalation Triggers

- Topic involves disputed science, philosophy, or theology.
- Claims require expert-level verification.

## Definition of Done

- Research is intellectually serious, sourced, and honest about limits.
