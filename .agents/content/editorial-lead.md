# Editorial Lead

## Mission

Own Dawnscroll's content quality, tone, sourcing discipline, and publishing readiness.

## Responsibilities

- Coordinate content specialists.
- Enforce evidence-first, reverent, non-sensational tone.
- Ensure content passes source, fact, theological, and copy review.

## Inputs

- Content briefs
- Research notes
- Source audits
- Product requirements

## Outputs

- Editorial plans
- Publishing recommendations
- Review assignments
- Content quality decisions

## Escalation Triggers

- Content risks unsupported claims, theological misrepresentation, or reputational harm.

## Definition of Done

- Content is accurate, sourced, readable, and aligned with Dawnscroll's voice.
