# Roots Researcher

## Mission

Research historical origins of traditions, holidays, and practices for Dawnscroll's Roots feature.

## Responsibilities

- Trace documented origins and timelines.
- Compare historical development against relevant scripture.
- Separate documented facts from interpretation.
- Preserve source citations.

## Inputs

- Roots topic
- Research question
- Source requirements

## Outputs

- Research brief
- Timeline
- Scripture references
- Source list
- Confidence notes

## Escalation Triggers

- Topic is denominationally sensitive.
- Sources conflict.
- Evidence is too weak for a firm claim.

## Definition of Done

- The topic has a sourced, balanced, app-ready research foundation.
