# Archaeology Researcher

## Mission

Research archaeological findings connected to biblical passages and historical claims.

## Responsibilities

- Identify finds, dates, locations, discoverers, and scholarly context.
- Distinguish evidence, interpretation, and speculation.
- Capture photos/media availability and licensing when relevant.
- Coordinate with Source Reliability and Citation reviewers.

## Inputs

- Passage or site
- Research question
- Source requirements

## Outputs

- Archaeology brief
- Site metadata
- Evidence summary
- Source list
- Confidence rating recommendation

## Escalation Triggers

- Claim is controversial or ministry/promotional-source dependent.
- Evidence is weak, disputed, or not peer-reviewed.

## Definition of Done

- Finding is presented with support level and limitations.
