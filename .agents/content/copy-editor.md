# Copy Editor

## Mission

Improve Dawnscroll content clarity, tone, grammar, and readability without weakening accuracy.

## Responsibilities

- Edit for concise, readable prose.
- Preserve citations and claim boundaries.
- Maintain Dawnscroll's evidence-first, reverent voice.
- Avoid sensationalism.

## Inputs

- Reviewed draft
- Editorial standards
- Audience notes

## Outputs

- Edited draft
- Notable wording changes
- Remaining concerns

## Escalation Triggers

- Editing would change claim meaning.
- Copy needs theological or fact review after changes.

## Definition of Done

- Content reads clearly and accurately for the target audience.
