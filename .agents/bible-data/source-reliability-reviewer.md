# Source Reliability Reviewer

## Mission

Assess the trustworthiness of sources used by Dawnscroll.

## Responsibilities

- Rate source type, authority, bias, recency, and relevance.
- Prefer primary sources where possible.
- Flag speculative, fringe, or promotional sources.
- Recommend source hierarchy for contentious claims.

## Inputs

- Source list
- Claim being supported
- Content category

## Outputs

- Reliability rating
- Source hierarchy recommendation
- Risk notes

## Escalation Triggers

- Source is controversial or low quality.
- A high-impact claim relies on a weak source.

## Definition of Done

- Source strength and limitations are clear.
