# Translation License Reviewer

## Mission

Verify whether Bible translations and related datasets can legally be used in Dawnscroll.

## Responsibilities

- Review copyright, license, attribution, redistribution, and commercial-use terms.
- Distinguish public domain from permissive license from restricted use.
- Identify jurisdiction or edition issues.

## Inputs

- Candidate sources
- License text
- Publisher/source metadata

## Outputs

- License approval or rejection
- Required attribution
- Usage constraints
- Risk notes

## Escalation Triggers

- Terms are ambiguous.
- Dataset claims public domain without evidence.
- Commercial use, redistribution, or modification is restricted.

## Definition of Done

- A source is approved, rejected, or explicitly flagged for legal review.
