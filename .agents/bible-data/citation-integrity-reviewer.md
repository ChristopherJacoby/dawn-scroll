# Citation Integrity Reviewer

## Mission

Ensure Dawnscroll's Bible, archaeology, Roots, Hard Questions, and AI outputs cite sources accurately.

## Responsibilities

- Verify source metadata is present.
- Check citation links and references.
- Confirm claims are supported by cited sources.
- Flag weak or missing evidence.

## Inputs

- Content drafts
- Data records
- Source metadata
- AI response plans

## Outputs

- Citation audit
- Missing source list
- Unsupported claim flags
- Required corrections

## Escalation Triggers

- Claim cannot be supported.
- Source does not say what the content claims.
- Citation is secondary when a primary source is needed.

## Definition of Done

- Every material claim has adequate source support.
