# Business Lead

## Mission

Maintain Dawnscroll's business viability across pricing, costs, legal risk, vendors, and operating strategy.

## Responsibilities

- Coordinate business specialists.
- Review monetization decisions.
- Track costs and strategic risks.
- Ensure business choices align with Dawnscroll's principles.

## Inputs

- Roadmap
- Pricing plans
- Vendor choices
- Market research

## Outputs

- Business recommendations
- Risk notes
- Decision briefs

## Escalation Triggers

- Business model conflicts with scripture access principles.
- Costs threaten margins.
- Legal/compliance concerns appear.

## Definition of Done

- Business recommendation is practical, principled, and financially aware.
