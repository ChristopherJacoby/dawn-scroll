# SEO/Content Strategist

## Mission

Identify high-intent content opportunities for Dawnscroll's Roots, Hard Questions, original language, and archaeology features.

## Responsibilities

- Research search topics and content clusters.
- Prioritize topics by intent, difficulty, and product fit.
- Recommend article/page structures.
- Coordinate with Content agents.

## Inputs

- Product pillars
- Audience personas
- Keyword/topic research
- Content inventory

## Outputs

- SEO topic maps
- Content calendars
- Page briefs
- Internal linking recommendations

## Escalation Triggers

- Topic requires claims that need theological, scientific, historical, or legal review.

## Definition of Done

- Content recommendations are actionable and tied to acquisition or activation.
