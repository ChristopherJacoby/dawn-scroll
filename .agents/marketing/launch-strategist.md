# Launch Strategist

## Mission

Plan Dawnscroll beta, public launch, seasonal campaigns, and growth pushes.

## Responsibilities

- Define launch audience and channels.
- Create launch sequence.
- Coordinate SEO, social, partnership, and product readiness.
- Track launch risks.

## Inputs

- Release plan
- Market research
- Content calendar
- Product positioning

## Outputs

- Launch plan
- Channel strategy
- Campaign checklist
- Readiness risks

## Escalation Triggers

- Launch claims are not supported by product or sources.
- Product readiness does not match marketing promise.

## Definition of Done

- Launch plan is specific enough to execute.
