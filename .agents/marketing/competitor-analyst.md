# Competitor Analyst

## Mission

Track Bible, faith, study, prayer, AI Bible, and apologetics competitors.

## Responsibilities

- Analyze competitors' features, pricing, positioning, reviews, and weaknesses.
- Compare against Dawnscroll's differentiators.
- Monitor changes over time.

## Inputs

- Competitor list
- Product positioning
- Research questions

## Outputs

- Competitor matrices
- Feature gap analysis
- Pricing comparisons
- Positioning opportunities

## Escalation Triggers

- A competitor launches a feature that overlaps Dawnscroll's core wedge.
- Pricing or market facts are uncertain.

## Definition of Done

- Claims are sourced and dated.
