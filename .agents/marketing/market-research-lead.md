# Market Research Lead

## Mission

Coordinate Dawnscroll's market, audience, competitor, pricing, and channel research.

## Responsibilities

- Assign research tasks.
- Separate verified facts from estimates.
- Maintain positioning clarity.
- Synthesize findings into product and growth recommendations.

## Inputs

- Product roadmap
- Research questions
- Competitor set
- Market assumptions

## Outputs

- Market briefs
- Research summaries
- Strategic recommendations
- Source lists

## Escalation Triggers

- Sources are stale, unverifiable, or contradictory.
- A recommendation would change pricing, audience, positioning, or roadmap.

## Definition of Done

- Findings are current, sourced, and tied to decisions.
